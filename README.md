# Credit Delinquency Risk Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

The Credit Delinquency Risk Analytics Dashboard is a tool that helps us understand how likely our customers are to miss their credit payments. This Credit Delinquency Risk Analytics Dashboard is made using Power BI. It is really interactive. The main goal of the Credit Delinquency Risk Analytics Dashboard is to find out which customers are at risk of missing their payments so we can keep an eye on them and make good decisions about credit risk.

## Business Problem

Banks and other financial institutions have a time figuring out which customers might miss their payments. If we can find out who these customers are on we can reduce our financial losses and make better plans to manage risk. The Credit Delinquency Risk Analytics Dashboard gives us a lot of information about how customers behave when it comes to missing payments, what kinds of risks they. How they pay their bills.

## Tools & Technologies

* We used Power BI to make the Credit Delinquency Risk Analytics Dashboard.

* We also used Power Query to get the data.

* DAX or Data Analysis Expressions helped us analyze the data.

* Data Visualization made it easier to understand the data.

* We used Business Intelligence to make decisions.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview Dashboard

This page gives us a look at the most important numbers:

* Total number of customers

* Number of customers who are missing their payments

* Percentage of customers who are missing their payments

* Average credit score of our customers

* Average income of our customers

* Average loan balance of our customers

### 2. Customer Delinquency Overview

This page analyzes:

* Which customers are missing their payments. Which are not

* How many customers are missing their payments in each area

* How delinquency varies by location

### 3. Risk Segmentation Dashboard

This page looks at:

* What kinds of risks our customers pose

* How delinquency changes based on the level of risk

* How credit score affects loan balance

### 4. Payment Behavior Analytics

This page tracks:

* How our customers pay their bills each month

* Which payments are on time late or missed

* How our customers are doing with their payments

### 5. Delinquency Drivers Dashboard

This page investigates:

* How having a job affects the risk of missing payments

* How credit score affects the risk of missing payments

* Which areas have the most customers missing their payments

## Key Insights

* Most of our customers are at risk of missing their payments.

* 16 percent of our customers are missing their payments.

* We see that customers are missing payments than they are making on time.

* Credit score and having a job are closely related to the risk of missing payments.

* Some areas have more customers missing their payments than others.

## Business Recommendations

* We should keep an eye on customers who are at medium risk of missing their payments.

* We should send reminders to our customers to pay their bills on time.

* We should be careful when giving credit to customers with credit scores.

* We should help customers who are consistently missing their payments.

## Project Outcomes

* We can now see clearly how our customers are doing with their credit.

* Our interactive dashboards help us keep an eye on delinquency.

* We have information to help us make good decisions, about risk and credit.
